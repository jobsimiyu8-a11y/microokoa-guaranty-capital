About Microokoa

We are a donation-driven Community Development Financial Institution (CDFI) that operates as a non-deposit-taking microfinance institution. This means we do not accept public deposits, ensuring maximum security for our community members.

Non-Deposit Taking

We don't accept public deposits - your money stays in our secured lending pool

Community Focused

Transparent Operations
